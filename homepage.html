Sierra Summits

Home Page

Sierra Summits is an online summit register. It allows users to access information on the 247 SPS peaks.

Users can sort and filter peaks based on milage, elevation or class.

After summiting a peak, users can sign the online summit register or leave comments for other users on trail conditions.

View Peaks

All peaks are shown in a thumbnail style fashion with the name of the peak, mileage and elevation gain

Each thumbnail can be clicked on for further information

View Specific Peak

Each individual peak will have its own page with a description and more detailed information.

Each peak will have its own summit log where users can sign or leave comments on conditions
